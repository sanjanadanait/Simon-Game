Simon Game - How to Play

  • Simon will give the first signal. (One of the Color Buttons will light up, accompanied by a tone.)
  • Repeat that signal by touching the same Color Button.
  • Simon will give another signal (Another of the Color Buttons will light up, accompanied by a tone.)
  • Repeat both the previous signals in the same order.
  • Simon will continue to play add more signals. Continue to repeat Simon in the same order for as long as you can!
  • If you fail to complete the sequence in the correct order, the Buzzer will sound and the game will end.
